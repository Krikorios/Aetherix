# Aetherix MSP Sales Narrative and Battlecard

Status: draft, May 2026. Audience: sales, partnerships, solutions engineering.

Use this as the baseline messaging for MSP discovery calls, partner onboarding,
and competitive positioning. Align all claims with current shipped scope.

## 1) Positioning Statement

Aetherix helps MSPs replace a stitched stack of endpoint tooling, GenAI-era DLP,
and compliance evidence collection with one deployable, tenant-aware platform.

## 2) Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

Best fit MSP profile:

- Serves SMB and lower mid-market customers with 25 to 1500 endpoints
- Operates managed security offerings with recurring service bundles
- Supports clients with SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, or similar obligations
- Sees growing ChatGPT/Claude/Gemini/Copilot usage among end users

Best fit end-customer profile (through MSP):

- Compliance-driven industries (healthcare, legal, finance, B2B SaaS)
- High sensitivity data handling and distributed workforce/browser-heavy workflows

## 3) Core Buyer Pains

- "We cannot reliably govern what users paste/upload into GenAI tools."
- "Audits consume too many engineering and account-management hours."
- "Our team is operating too many consoles and agents for the same client."
- "Margin is tight because service delivery is not standardized."

## 4) Value Proposition (Customer-Facing)

- Unified operations: fewer agents and fewer consoles to manage
- GenAI-aware controls: browser session coverage where modern leakage occurs
- Compliance acceleration: evidence output from real security telemetry
- MSP alignment: multi-tenant packaging and customer-level policy control

## 5) 30-Second Pitch

"You already manage endpoint risk, data movement risk, and audit risk, but with
separate tools and workflows. Aetherix unifies these controls into one MSP-first
platform, adds GenAI browser guardrails where sensitive data now leaks, and
produces auditor-ready evidence from native events instead of manual screenshots."

## 6) Discovery Questions

Use these in first meetings:

1. How many endpoint, DLP, browser, and compliance tools are in your current stack?
2. Which customer segments drive the most audit or evidence-prep overhead?
3. How is GenAI usage currently governed across client environments?
4. Where do you lose time due to multi-console investigation workflows?
5. What would a 25-40% audit-prep reduction mean for your service margin?

## 7) Objection Handling

"We already have EDR."

- Response: Keep your current posture where needed; Aetherix's wedge is unified
  GenAI-aware DLP plus compliance evidence workflow tied to endpoint events.

"This sounds early."

- Response: Correct; we run design-partner pilots with transparent scope and
  explicit success criteria before broad rollout.

"We use Microsoft security already."

- Response: Aetherix targets MSP operating simplicity across customers and offers
  a unified policy/evidence plane for managed delivery.

## 8) Competitive Battlecard (High-Level)

### vs Coro / Guardz

- Their strength: MSP-first packaging and broad SMB security footprint
- Our angle: native policy and evidence model spanning endpoint + GenAI browser
  controls + compliance output in one chain
- Talk track: reduce dependence on stitched third-party engines over time while
  improving control/evidence consistency

### vs Defender/Purview-heavy stack

- Their strength: deep Microsoft ecosystem integration
- Our angle: simpler MSP-facing packaging and cross-surface policy experience
- Talk track: reduce SKU and workflow fragmentation in multi-customer operations

### vs CrowdStrike / SentinelOne class

- Their strength: mature endpoint depth and market credibility
- Our angle: MSP operational unification with GenAI browser DLP and integrated
  compliance evidence generation
- Talk track: sell service efficiency and audit outcome, not only endpoint depth

### vs Compliance-only automation platforms

- Their strength: GRC workflow and questionnaire/evidence management
- Our angle: evidence generated from security-native telemetry, not only third-
  party integrations and manual controls collection

## 9) Packaging and Upsell Motion

- `Core`: baseline managed endpoint + incident operations
- `+GenAI` (Core add-on): browser guardrails and destination-aware semantic DLP
- `+CompliancePro` (Core add-on): framework-aligned evidence workflows and exports

Expansion sequence:

1. Land on Core for standard managed protection
2. Expand into +GenAI for high-usage knowledge-workforce customers
3. Expand into +CompliancePro for audit-driven accounts
